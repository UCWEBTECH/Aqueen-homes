Description

Located on the Chandapura Anekal Road, Nakshatra brings you the joys of living in expansive open and fresh spaces while remaining in close proximily to Electronic City with major IT, Biotech & MNC firms. Facing 100 feet main road and sprawling over 28 acres of land, Nakshatra Township consists of plots of various sizes amidst Villas & Villa Plots. It sits adjacent to the flourshing KHB surya city and right opposite to Alliance University. The location is prime with links and easy access to Electronic city, Sarjapur Road, Jigani link Road and Bannerghatta Road, to fulfil all your needs, Schools, Hospitals, Restaurants etc.. Nakshatra Township is all set to see rapidly developing progress and the buzz of a rising and sought after residential hub.

Nakshatra Township is a one of a kind, BMRDA Approved Residential Layout on Chandapur-Anekal belt, Comprising of 480 units of Villas and Villa plots spread across 28 acres of land under phase-1. This prime yet affordable land on the shores of one of the fastest developing areas in south Bangalore.
