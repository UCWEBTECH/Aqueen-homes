Pionier Lifestyle Plot Sarjapur Road Overview

Pionier Lifestyle Plot by Pionier Developers is a gated development project set on the banks of a scenic lake, and surrounded with lush greenery. It offers plots of different sizes, such as 30x40, 30x45, 30x50, 30x55. The project offers for its residents a fine blend of modern amenities and natural greenery. With a central park and 3 natural water bodies, synthetic-floored tennis and basket-ball courts, amphitheatre, gym, spa, restaurants, skating rink, child’s pool, day care area and lots more! Situated next to Bangalore’s growth corridor, international schools, colleges, hospitals and more it is an ideal destination where all your needs are close to home.
